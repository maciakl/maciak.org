Bookmarklet

Drag the following link to the bookmarks bar:

DE-HULKIFY

What is this?

If you like reading Film Critic Hulk, but you can't stand the ALL CAPS this bookmarklet will re-capitalize the entire text for you for easier consumption.

WHEN YOU CLICK THIS BOOKMARKLET, ALL THE TEXT ON THE PAGE SHOULD BE TRANSFORMED TO LOWER-CASE SO IT DOESN'T FEEL LIKE SOMEONE IS SHOUTING DAGGERS AT YOUR EYES.
